A bit about me

I was born in Mexico in 2002 and completed all my education within the French system, which provided me with a rich bicultural education. At the age of 7, I discovered my passion for building Legos, an activity that nurtured my creativity and interest in structures and systems. Around the age of 12, this passion evolved into a fascination with the virtual world, sparking my interest in technology and its endless potential.

After a pause in my educational journey due to the pandemic, I decided to relocate to France to pursue higher education. During my professional experience at Communica, I discovered a field that deeply inspires me: data management and analysis.

Today, I am passionate about the world of data. I am actively engaged in learning its professional applications and striving to deepen my skills to contribute to innovative and strategic projects in this constantly evolving field.
