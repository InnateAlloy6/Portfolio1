Photography

In my photography journey, I started at the age of 10, experimenting with my first camera on ants.

A few years later, I discovered street photography and began capturing images of Mexico City.

Eventually, during my studies at the Lycée Franco-Mexicain, I had the opportunity to get certified by Canon Academy, where I developed a liking for macro and urban photography.

Today, not only have I been fortunate enough to travel the world, but I also enjoy going out to the streets to take interesting photos of the city, people, and objects.

For me, photography is a way to capture a vision from an unusual perspective and find a different world and story in the same world we know.

Dungeons & Dragons

I discovered the role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons by chance during the pandemic, and it quickly became a significant part of my life. As the organizer of the gaming table, this hobby has allowed me to develop leadership, organizational, and group management skills.

This game has also been a source of personal growth, helping me express my emotions and reignite my creativity. It has given me the opportunity to design detailed worlds, tell legendary stories, and create captivating adventures. Furthermore, Dungeons & Dragons has enriched my journey by enabling me to forge new friendships while exploring imaginary worlds.
